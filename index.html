About the Photographer

Capturing the essence of Uganda, one frame at a time.

With over a decade of experience in visual storytelling, Lenz Photography specializes in blending traditional aesthetics with modern visual techniques. Based in the heart of Kampala, our style is characterized by warm tones, authentic emotion, and a relentless pursuit of the perfect light.

Whether it's a high-fashion portrait session or an intimate wedding celebration, we believe every subject has a unique narrative. My background in photojournalism ensures that no candid moment goes unnoticed, while my studio expertise guarantees flawlessly lit portraits.
